Designing a Shopping app

I've been shopping online for quiet a while now, but the experience is almost always very mechanical and lacks personal touch , hence I set out to design an alternate app that creates a unique shopping experience keeping viewer's interest in mind.

Welcome Page

The onboarding process is kept pretty simple. It has been used to familiairize users to the features they will be using in this app.

It has been seen that a lot of users bounce off apps due to long registration processes. So in order to solve this the skip button is included during registration to take the user straight to the home page , and gives them a taste of the app.

Interests of people are taken into considerarion to give valuable recomendations and increasing the buying rate of users

The form is divided into small section , so that it is easy for the user to fill it out.
